    History

When first brainstorming the name for the company back in 2012 there was a solid descion to name the company Trickster Inc. but... later it turned out that was an alredy established. So, the founder Kuradou Ansoni was determined to come up with a fresh new but simple to speak company name. It took a while to come up with a decent meaningful name that hasn't been taken at all across the internet. The founder nearly gave up till the middle of 2018 after plenty of research and thought the idea for name was hatched, Sekai Play!
